Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

1. What is PaperPilot?

PaperPilot is the ultimate reference management tool designed to help you effortlessly manage and share your research papers.

2. How can I get started?

To get started, you do not need to do anything! In case you would like to add a paper or present a Paper Reading Round (PRR) you will have to LogIn with your official THI credentianls.

3. Can I use PaperPilot for collaboration?

Yes, PaperPilot provides powerful collaboration features (upcoming!). You can share papers with other researchers, participate in discussions, and stay updated on the latest research.

Managing Papers

4. How do I add a new paper?

To add a new paper, navigate to the "Add Paper" section and fill in the required information such as title, author, and publication details. One more hint, if you provide a valid DOI or arXiv number the information will be filled out automatically for you!

5. Can I upload attachments with my papers?

Yes, you can upload attachments when adding or updating a paper. Supported file types include PDF, supplementary materials, and more.

6. What is the process for updating or deleting a paper?

You can update or delete a paper by navigating to the respective options in the user dashboard. Make sure to confirm your actions, especially when deleting a paper, as it cannot be undone.

Paper Reading Rounds (PRRs)

7. What are Paper Reading Rounds (PRRs)?

PRRs are collaborative sessions where researchers present and discuss papers. It's a great way to exchange ideas, get feedback, and stay informed about the latest research in your field.

8. How can I participate in a PRR?

To participate in a PRR, check the upcoming events section. If you find a PRR that interests you, you can join the session, contribute to the discussion, or even volunteer to present a paper.

Contact and Support

9. How can I contact support?

For support, please send an email to panos.pagonis@thi.de.

Report Bugs or Suggest Features

10. Where can I report bugs or suggest features?

To report bugs or suggest features, please visit our issue tracker on GitLab.
